Nascar Authentics 1:64

NASCAR Authentics 1:64 cars are the standard retail diecast cars sold at stores like Walmart and Target. These cars are released in numbered “waves” each year, with every wave containing a specific lineup of drivers, paint schemes, and occasionally rare chase versions. This category represents the core of the NASCAR collection and is the most consistent and achievable series to complete.

Nascar Authentics 1:64 Gold Series

NASCAR Authentics 1:64 Gold Series cars are a premium version of the standard Authentics line. They feature enhanced details such as metallic or chrome-style finishes, upgraded wheels, and special paint treatments. Gold Series cars are released in waves just like the standard line but are produced in smaller quantities, making them more limited and harder to find.

Disney Pixar Cars Singles 1:64

Disney Pixar Cars Singles 1:64 are individual diecast character cars based on the Cars movie franchise. These are sold individually at major retailers and are released throughout the year in case assortments. This category includes main characters, background racers, and new character debuts, making it ideal for tracking a growing and constantly evolving collection.

Disney Pixar Cars Themed Series

Disney Pixar Cars Themed Series consist of specially branded collections that group cars around a shared theme, such as specific locations, events, seasons, or visual styles. These series are clearly labeled on packaging and are released as complete sets, making them perfect for checklist-style collecting and easy progress tracking.
